Riftshards of Apollyon: Campaign


In a world fractured by the unpredictable forces of rifts, reality itself is under siege. Mysterious rifts, imbued with volatile Archonium crystals, open across the lands, threatening to release eldritch horrors into the material plane. Rift hunters are all that stand between civilization and annihilation, using their skills to track down these rifts and neutralize the creatures within before the rift collapsesâ€”and the entities within are unleashed.

Players will be thrust into a world of escalating chaos, as rival cities wage war over control of the rifts, each hoping to harness its power for their own ends. Seongjeong, your home base, stands as a neutral hub of knowledge and training, but as the rifts grow more numerous and dangerous, alliances will be tested, and survival is never guaranteed.

As Rift Hunters, you must learn to navigate political intrigue, face unimaginable cosmic horrors, and confront the very nature of reality itself. Only by mastering the mysteries of the rifts can you hope to protect the world from ultimate destruction.
